About me

An avid follower of different sports with a strong belief in the healing power of movement. Love everything about football ("soccer" for my American friends). Enjoy partaking in different types of physical activities, reading a good book, watching (mostly) the same movies again and again. And of course, hanging out with close friends and indulging in unhealthy food every once in a while.

On a more professional note, I enjoy working with people and helping them achieve their health and fitness goals.
